About

I am a junior at Cornell University studying Computer Science in the College of Engineering. I am Vice President of Cornell Business Analytics, a student-run organization that offers pro-bono business analytics consulting to a wide variety of clients. Some of the projects I have I have worked on in the past include creating an interactive web-app for a major yogurt company and building a time series forecasting model to predict seasonal demand for an American food company. I also am a member of the Social Dynamics Lab directed by Professor Michael Macy, where I do research in Computational Social Science under Alex Ruch. Currently, I am extracting the emotional arcs of users on Reddit over time and studying their mental health dynamics as well as increasing or decreasing risk of suicidality.
